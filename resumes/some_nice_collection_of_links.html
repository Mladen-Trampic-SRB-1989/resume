  click me  Welcome to the AWS security workshops portal! Here you will find a collection of workshops and other hands-on content aimed at helping you gain an understanding of the AWS service ecosystem and introduce you to a variety of best practices that can be applied to securing your environments and workloads running in AWS.
Workshops around security grouped in areas such as Identity, Data Protection, Infrastructure Security, Detection and Response, DevSecOps
